About Echelon

Echelon is the premier management festival of St. Aloysius University, Mangalore. It is a dynamic platform designed to challenge the brightest minds in business strategy, marketing, finance, and human resources. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application. Echelon provides an unparalleled opportunity for students to network with industry stalwarts, showcase their talents, and prepare for the corporate world.
