Brainwave

About the Event

Brainwave is the ultimate idea-pitching competition. Participants are challenged to present groundbreaking and innovative ideas to a panel of judges. It's a platform to bring your visionary concepts to life and compete for amazing prizes.

Rules

  • Each participant/team gets 5 minutes to present, followed by a 3-minute Q&A.
  • Presentations must be accompanied by a slide deck.
  • Ideas will be judged based on originality, feasibility, and market potential.

Prizes

Cash prizes, mentorship opportunities, and more for the top 3 ideas!

#Ctrl+Alt+Escape

About the Event

#Ctrl+Alt+Escape is a competitive debate where participants sharpen their arguments and public speaking skills. Debaters will be given topics on the spot, challenging them to think on their feet and deliver persuasive arguments.

Structure

  • Classic 1-on-1 parliamentary debate format.
  • Topics will be from current affairs, technology, and ethics.
  • Participants are judged on content, clarity, and rhetorical skill.

Who can participate?

Individual participation is required.

ReelGear

About the Event

ReelGear is a 24-hour hackathon where teams collaborate to build innovative solutions for real-world problems. It's a test of creativity, technical skill, and teamwork under pressure. Code, design, and build something amazing!

Themes

  • Sustainable Technology
  • Health & Wellness
  • Education Technology

Requirements

Bring your own laptop and gear. Food, drinks, and a great coding environment will be provided.
