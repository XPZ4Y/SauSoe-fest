About Soar Fest

Soar Fest is an annual technical and cultural festival that is held in St Aloysius University, Mangalore. Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and learning. From intense coding marathons to mind-bending robotics challenges, Soar Fest is the platform to showcase your skills, learn from experts, and network with peers who share your passion.

This year, we're pushing the boundaries further with new event categories, esteemed guest speakers, and bigger prizes. Get ready for an unforgettable experience that will challenge, inspire, and elevate you.
